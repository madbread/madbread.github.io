Mad Bread is a Chicago folk band that plays a unique mixture of bluegrass, rock and blues. A string band for the 21st century

At a concert, you'll hear them move effortlessly from boot-stompin' bluegrass jams to Chicago-style blues-rock with stops everywhere in between.

When performing, the band's philosophy is simple: the audience should enjoy listening as much as the band enjoys playing. It's all about having a good time.
