"Southport & Eddy" is the band's highly anticipated debut album. Featuring 13 original songs. The album takes you on a sonic journey through space and time, modernity and tradition, war and peace, love and loss.

Acoustic and electric guitars blend seamlessly with the more ancient tones of the banjo and mandolin. Mad Bread is traditional and groundbreaking all at once.

You can download all the tracks from the album as mp3's above. Full-quality sound is available on the CD.
